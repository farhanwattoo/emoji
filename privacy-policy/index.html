Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Emoji Kitchen handles browser-side tool use, optional third-party links, and any information a visitor may choose to share.

Most tools on this site are designed to run directly in the browser. In those cases, the site may not need to store the information a visitor enters in order to deliver the main utility shown on the page.

Some pages may reference third-party services or links, such as external support pages or utility resources. When a visitor opens those services, their privacy practices are governed by the destination site rather than by this page alone.

If analytics, advertising, or embedded third-party scripts are added in the future, this policy should be updated to explain what is collected, why it is collected, and what choices visitors have.
